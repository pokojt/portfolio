Portland-based web developer, musician, and ice cream enthusiast.

Originally from small-town Illinois, Taylor moved to Oregon in 2014, chasing misty forests and connection with like-minded weirdos. While she holds her BA in Music and is currently working on a Certificate of Web Development and Design, Taylor is enamored with many other subject areas; her determination is to learn as much as she can about ...everything. She has lived in many different places and thrives on learning new things from new people. With a combination of her creative background and her scientific mind, Taylor will bring a fresh perspective, an insatiable curiosity, and a calm energy wherever she goes.

Web Development

Taylor is currently enrolled in a web development bootcamp at Epicodus in downtown Portland. Though she is just getting started with programming basics, Taylor is interested in pursuing a track to become a front-end software developer. Check out the Projects page to see what she's been working on!

Music

Taylor earned her music degree from Columbia College Chicago, with a focus in vocal performance and music production. Though voice is her main instrument, Taylor also plays some piano, guitar, ukulele, and accordion, with a strong desire to learn the cello. Taylor enjoys writing, mixing, and producing her original music and aspires to one day form a world-fusion group. She also would love to use her web development skills to build a platform for original music collaboration.

Wellness

One of Taylor's biggest goals is to help other people feel happy and fulfilled. She hopes to begin work in Energy Healing in the near future and complete her certificate to become a Reiki Master Practicioner.

A strong believer in nature's medicine, Taylor spends as much time as possible hiking, camping, and looking closely at moss.
